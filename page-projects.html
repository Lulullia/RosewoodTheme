Stories
Project Circus.
The first story to be released; a practice project to prepare for the next two behemoths.

Follow the adventures of a group of performers by day and criminals by night, travelling the world of Lithlotka in their quest for freedom.

Project Lone.
The second story, a sort of bridge between Project Circus and Lyphuriaa, while still being very different.

The times are tough for Lyphurians still on Devonian soil. The war may be over, but the fear of getting killed is far from gone. This is the stage where a Lyphurian slave, a fake princess and a Vampire assassin get to play their story.

Lyphuriaa.
The third story, the one I wanted to create from the beginning. Everything that comes before leads to this.

Lyphuriaa is still as dead as ever... and no one knows what disastrous consequences that might imply for the world.
